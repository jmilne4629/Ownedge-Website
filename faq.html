Frequently Asked Questions

What is OWNEDGE?

OWNEDGE is a comprehensive training platform designed for athletes. We combine proven programming with EdgeAI to provide personalised programs across strength, power, endurance, speed, fascia health and recovery.

What do I get with the Freemium plan?

The Freemium plan grants you a four‑week personalised program and progress tracking – completely free. It’s perfect for testing out the platform.

What does Pro include?

Pro unlocks every feature we offer: unlimited program adaptations, detailed analytics, full access to EdgeAI insights and workouts across all pillars.

How do I subscribe via Stripe?

Simply choose your plan on the Pricing page and click Subscribe. You will be redirected to a secure Stripe Checkout where you can enter payment details. After confirmation you’ll receive access immediately.

Does EdgeAI adapt my workouts?

Yes. EdgeAI analyses your training data and performance metrics to adapt your workouts, ensuring you always train at the appropriate intensity and volume.
